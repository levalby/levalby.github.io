Digital Communication Analysis

Implemented MATLAB simulations to evaluate Bit Error Rate (BER) performance in AWGN and Rayleigh fading channels using BPSK, 4-PSK, and 8-PSK. Developed and analyzed Maximum Likelihood and Minimum Distance decoders, and assessed the impact of convolutional coding with Viterbi decoding on BER. Conducted Monte-Carlo simulations to compare theoretical and practical results, optimizing spectral efficiency and error correction techniques.
